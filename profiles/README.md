# EHFGM Media OBS Profiles

These profiles are created for church livestreaming and recording workflows.

## What is an OBS Profile?

An OBS Profile stores output and streaming settings such as:

- Resolution
- FPS
- Bitrate
- Encoder settings
- Audio settings
- Recording settings
- Streaming service settings

Profiles do not store scenes, cameras, overlays, images, or lyrics sources. Those are stored in Scene Collections.

## Included Profiles

### EHFGM - 720p30 Low Internet

For churches with unstable or low upload speed.

Recommended upload speed: minimum 5 Mbps stable upload.

### EHFGM - 720p30 Standard

Default recommended profile for most churches.

Recommended upload speed: minimum 8 Mbps stable upload.

### EHFGM - 1080p30 Good Internet

For churches with stable high upload speed.

Recommended upload speed: minimum 12 Mbps stable upload.

### EHFGM - Recording Only

For high-quality local recording without livestreaming.
